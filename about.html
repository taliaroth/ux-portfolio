My Approach

Adaptable Process

I follow a flexible framework inspired by the double diamond methodology and lean UX principles, tailoring my approach to each project's unique needs and constraints

Research-Driven

I conduct research to thoroughly understand the problem space before moving to solution development which is especially helpful for riskier projects

Collaborative Design

I prioritize cross-functional collaboration throughout the design process, working closely with product managers, developers, and stakeholders to ensure designs are both innovative and implementable

Creative Ideation

I place special emphasis on the ideation phase, facilitating exploration of multiple solutions to uncover unexpected approaches that often prove more feasible and user-friendly

Strategic Prioritization

I assess each project's scope and risk level to strategically distribute my time across different design phases, focusing resources where they'll have the greatest impact

Balanced Solutions

I balance creative exploration with practical constraints to deliver designs that create delightful user experiences while aligning with business objectives and technical realities
